FUD PORTAL

Welcome to the Federal University Dutse portal. All services on this portal are restricted,
hence, only authenticated users can access them. Please login.

Students should use their Registration numbers as both usernames and passwords when
logging in for the first time.

For all enquiries and/or complaints regarding Registration, send an email to
ictsupport@fud.edu.ng

FRESH STUDENT REGISTRATION GUIDELINES

  1. All School fees Registration must be through this Portal and NOT THROUGH
    REMITA WEBSITE
  2. Once verified by the Head of Department, log onto this portal with your JAMB
    Registration Number     as Username and STATE OF ORIGIN (in CAPITAL letters) as
    Password.
  3. Click School Fees to generate payment invoice, click Remita logo to Pay online with
    a debit card or print invoice receipt to pay at FUD Microfinance or any commercial
    Bank.
  4. Proceed to the respective Faculty Accountant Office for submission of a copy of
    Remita Payment receipt for stamp and verification.
  5. After payment, click Generate REGISTRATION NUMBER to generate your
    Registration Number.
  6. CLICK GO TO SCHOOL to logout and use your newly generated REGISTRATION
    NUMBER     as Username and Password to login.
  7. The system will prompt you to change your password, and make sure your
    password is strong (Combination of letters, numbers, and special characters)
  8. Upload your Passport and select Biodata to update your biodata
  9. Print Clinic form to proceed to the University Clinic for Lab test.
  10. Course Registration to register courses. Students are strongly advised to
    seek guidance from their respective level coordinators on the courses they are
    supposed to register.
  11. To apply for accommodation, click on Hostel to reserve bed space.

Note: Only Fresh and Final year students are eligible for accommodation, and the
allocation of bed spaces in both male and Female Hostel will be done on a first-come-first-
served basis.
